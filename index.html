About Me

Simon is a cybersecurity expert with 10 years of experience, specializing in offensive security for the past 9 years. As a Senior Advisor at Desjardins, he focuses on high-impact attack chains. He has conducted over 250 penetration tests and is an active member of the cybersecurity community, dedicated to sharing his knowledge by volunteering at events like Hackfest CTF and training individuals for over 5 years in the Security 103 course. Simon values honesty, transparency, and integrity in his work. His expertise covers network infrastructure, Active Directory pentesting, red teaming, access control evasion, and efficient computer use. He constantly strives to improve business security by writing precise reports and providing actionable recommendations to enhance resilience against adversaries. He believes that many root causes of security issues can be addressed through knowledge and training. His goal is to educate and strengthen security measures to protect businesses and individuals in digital environments.
